FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is SaveFrom.net and why do you need it?
SaveFrom.net makes downloading from the internet convenient and simple. With our help you can download audio, video, and other types of files from various websites and social networks: youtube.com, vk.com, vimeo.com, and others.
How to use the service?
  • 1st way: Enter the URL-address in the input field at the top of the main page and press Enter or click on the button to the right of the input field.
  • 2nd way: Add "savefrom.net/" or "sfrom.net/" before the URL and press Enter. example: sfrom.net/http://youtube.com/watch?v=u7deClndzQw
  • 3rd way: Use short domain names: ssyoutube.com, srapidshare.com.
  • 4th way: Use our browser addon to download in 1 click.
  • Note: Click on a web resource name to see the details of downloading and saving files from it.
How to install SaveFrom.net helper on your computer?
Download the installer from our website, close your browser, run the installer and follow the instructions. You can install the extension in your browser. In order to do this, follow the link, choose your browser from the list and click on the link. Our extension supports Google Chrome, Yandex.Browser, Mozilla Firefox, Opera, Safari, Chromium. You can always disable the extension on any website. Just go to the extension settings and click "Disable on this website".
